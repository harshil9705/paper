ABOUT US

SHIVAM PAPERS PRODUCTS

Who We Are?

Shivam Paper Products is a leading manufacturer and exporter of paper yarn cones, paper tubes in India. With years of expertise in the paper and packaging industry, we cater to both domestic and international markets, offering premium-quality, durable, and eco-friendly paper solutions for various industries especially the textile, packaging, and industrial sectors.

WHY CHOOSE SHIVAM PAPER PRODUCTS ?

Premium Quality Materials: We use high-grade kraft paper, adhesives, inks & chemicals from trusted suppliers for superior strength and sustainability.

Customized Machinery for Client Needs: Our production machinery is upgraded and tailored to Indian and overseas client needs for better performance and accuracy.

Strict Quality Control: Expert shift managers supervise every stage with continuous testing to maintain international standards.

Technical Support: Our skilled team provides on-site guidance to ensure smooth running of our cones and tubes on customer machines.

Timely & Reliable Delivery: Consistent quality, competitive pricing, and strong long-term customer relationships worldwide.



Global Presence

Shivam Paper Products supplies paper yarn cones, paper tubes, and paper cores to customers in the USA, UK, Egypt, Iran, Brazil, Benin, and the United Arab Emirates. We are growing our export network throughout Asia, Africa, and the Middle East, providing reliable and eco-friendly paper products for the textile and packaging industries around the world.

Our Vision
