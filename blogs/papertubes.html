What Are Paper Tubes?

Paper tubes are cylindrical structures created by winding multiple layers of kraft paper or cardboard under high pressure. These custom paper tubes are strong, lightweight, and 100% recyclable ‚Äî making them a sustainable choice for a wide range of industrial and commercial applications.

Types, Uses, and Creation of Paper Tubes

  • Textile Industry: Used for yarn winding, thread rolling, and fabric support.
  • Packaging & Labels: Ideal as cores for adhesive tapes, flexible films, and labels.
  • Construction: Heavy-duty concrete form tubes used for cylindrical pillars and molds.
  • Food & Cosmetics: Safe, eco-friendly packaging for tea, coffee, snacks, and premium goods.

Paper tubes serve multiple industries ‚Äî from textiles to food and packaging.

Why Choose Paper Tubes?

Choosing packaging paper tubes over plastic or metal alternatives helps reduce waste and carbon footprint. They are biodegradable, recyclable, and cost-effective, making them ideal for packing and shipping applications that demand sustainability without compromising strength or appearance.
