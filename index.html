PAPER YARN CONES

Paper Yarn Cones are an eco-friendly, durable, and lightweight solution made from high-quality paper fibers, offering a sustainable alternative to traditional and synthetic yarns. At Shivam Paper Products, we specialize in manufacturing premium paper yarn cones designed for strength, flexibility, and long-lasting performance. Widely used in textiles, weaving, knitting, crafts, decor, and packaging industries, our paper yarn combines smooth texture, high tensile strength, and moisture resistance to ensure superior functionality. Available in various thicknesses, colors, and finishes, these yarn cones are fully biodegradable and recyclable, supporting sustainable production while maintaining high-quality standards. Choose Shivam Paper Products for reliable, customizable, and eco-conscious paper yarn cones that contribute to a greener future.

PAPER TUBE

Paper Tubes are strong, lightweight, and eco-friendly cylindrical cores made from premium-quality paperboard, widely used in textiles, packaging, construction, and paper industries. At Shivam Paper Products, we manufacture high-strength and precise paper tubes known for their durability, load-bearing capacity, and smooth finish. Ideal for yarn winding, film rolling, and protective packaging, our tubes are biodegradable, recyclable, and customizable in various sizes, thicknesses, and coatings. They offer excellent moisture and heat resistance, providing a sustainable and reliable alternative to plastic and metal tubes for modern industrial applications.
